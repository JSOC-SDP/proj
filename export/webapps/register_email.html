JSOC Export E-Mail Registry

JSOC Data Export Email Registry

JSOC data exports require an email address to be validated before an export request can be submitted. This page can be used to pre-register an email address so that future export requests using the registered email address can proceed without interruption. Please enter the email address you will use to be notified of completed data export requests in the "Notify" box.

If you have a current SolarMail address you may put "solarmail" in the Notify box (no quotes) put your SolarMail name in the "Requester" box.

If you are not using SolarMail for data export notification then you may leave the Requester field blank or fill it with your name so we can use it in any communication with you if you have problems with export requests or if we need to notify you of any problems with the JSOC export service.

After entering any needed information in the two boxes below, press the "Submit" button. If your address is already registered with the JSOC you will get a rapid confirmation and no mail will be sent to you at this time.

If your address is not in our list then you will recieve email within a short time asking you to simply "Reply" to that message with no changes needed to the message. When we recieve the reply your address will be registered.

If we do not recieve a reply message within three minutes the registration attempt will be stopped. If the address is correct but you could not reply in time, simply try again.

If you have "Cookies" enabled in your browser we will save the Notify and Requester values and preload them into future JSOC export requests until the Cookie times out after 10 days with no use.
